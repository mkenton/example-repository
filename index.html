Welcome to my first web creation. I am excited to build knowledge at FlatIron to apply to furture endeavors. As a start, I will share my proudest collaborative creation - the Night Lily. While a website (let alone my very first) probably can't capture its full glory, I am employing my "Prework" learnings in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to give you a small taste of what it is like to visit the Night Lily.

Night Lily Transformation

The Night Lily is far from a static object. During the day, its white fabric glistens in the sunlight. It takes the form of a bud, which occasioanlly blooms into a full 12-foot flower. It also can surprise its visitors with a soothing mist. At night, the Lily blooms into a visual explosion of light, color, and motion.

Never the Same Pattern

In my work on the the Night Lily, I combined animations created by my team with some effects and video techniques to create mesmerizing textures. Rather than show a pre-rendered video on a timed loop, I worked to create a visual display that never showed the exact same pattern twice. The results spoke for themselves - we watched festival-goers take much-needed breaks from the ruckus, and spend minutes to sometimes hours meditatively watching the ever-changing, hypnotizing content. Below is an image configured to animate the Night Lily when you hover over it with your cursor. I hope by using this feature you can begin to imagine the experience of visiting our ever-evolving Night Lily creation.
